========================
UGC Script 1
========================

Scene: Shot on a smartphone. The speaker is standing in a casual but professional setting (like a home office or a well-lit cafe). They look directly into the lens. Text overlays appear on screen to emphasize key points.

(0–3 sec) Hook
Visual: Speaker leans in slightly, looking confident.
Audio: "If your Instagram has 2,000 followers but you’re still getting zero calls every day, I’m talking to you."
Text Overlay: 👇 Zero calls? (Hindi: Zero calls?)

(3–10 sec) Problem
Visual: Speaker gestures as if explaining to a friend.
Audio: "You’re probably posting 'happy Diwali' posts and photos of your products. The problem? Nobody buys from a gallery. They buy from a brand they trust."
Text Overlay: Problem: Posting like a gallery.

(10–20 sec) Solution
Visual: Speaker points to the corner of the screen as if revealing a secret.
Audio: "At [Agency Name], we don’t just 'post' for you. We build a content strategy that turns scrollers into paying customers. We use Indian storytelling to build trust so your DMs actually flood."
Text Overlay: We build trust. You get sales.

(20–25 sec) Benefits / Results
Visual: Speaker smiles, relaxed.
Audio: "Imagine waking up to DMs from people who are ready to buy, while you focus on running your business."
Text Overlay: DMs while you sleep.

(25–30 sec) CTA
Visual: Speaker points down to the comment section or bio link.
Audio: "We have 5 spots open for consultation this week. Check the link in my bio to book yours."
Text Overlay: Link in Bio. (Hindi: Free consultation ke liye link in bio)

Production Notes for the Creator:

Pacing: Keep the energy slightly higher than a normal conversation, but not "salesy." Think of it as giving a friend a solid tip.

Language: Use English as the base, but drop in Hinglish words like "bilkul," "yaar," or "fayda" in the ad-libs to feel more relatable to the Indian SMB.

Background Music: Use a trending, low-fi beat that is popular on Reels right now, kept low enough that the voice is crystal clear.

UGC Script 2

The "Analytics Deep Dive" (Authority/Vanity Trigger)
This hook appeals to business owners who check their insights daily but don't understand them.

(0–3 sec) Hook
Visual: Speaker holds phone up showing the Instagram "Professional Dashboard" with a low engagement rate.
Audio: "Your reach dropped 40% last week. Don't blame the algorithm. Blame your content strategy."
Text Overlay: 📉 -40% Reach? Here’s why.

(3–10 sec) Problem
Visual: Speaker puts phone down, looks at camera seriously.
Audio: "You're reposting random Reels from other pages and hoping for the best. Instagram sees this as 'unoriginal content' and stops showing you to new people."
Text Overlay: Stop reposting. It kills reach.

(10–20 sec) Solution
Visual: Speaker gestures to self/agency.
Audio: "At [Agency Name], we audit your page first. We find exactly what's broken—whether it's your hashtags, your timing, or your hook—and we fix it with a custom Indian audience strategy."
Text Overlay: We fix what's broken.

(20–25 sec) Benefits / Results
Visual: Speaker mimics scrolling and stopping suddenly.
Audio: "Result? New customers walking into your store or clinic saying, 'I saw you on Instagram.'"
Text Overlay: Real customers. Real stories.

(25–30 sec) CTA
Visual: Speaker holds hand out as if offering something.
Audio: "I'm doing free audits for 5 business owners this week. Link in bio to claim yours."
Text Overlay: Free Audit 👆 Link in Bio

The "Money Left on the Table" (Fear/Greed Trigger)
This hook targets business owners who are losing customers to competitors who are more active online.

(0–3 sec) Hook
Visual: Speaker looks concerned/empathetic.
Audio: "If you're a local salon or restaurant in India and you're not on Reels, you're losing ₹50,000 a month. Minimum."
Text Overlay: Lakhpati bane? (Becoming a lakhpati?)

(3–10 sec) Problem
Visual: Speaker gestures like they are counting money.
Audio: "Your competitor down the street is getting all the weekend bookings because people found them on the Explore page. You? You're relying on 'walk-ins.'"
Text Overlay: Don't rely on walk-ins.

(10–20 sec) Solution
Visual: Speaker points confidently.
Audio: "We don't just manage your page; we run it like a business. We create content that shows your best food, your best service, and gets people excited to visit you."
Text Overlay: We show your best side.

(20–25 sec) Benefits / Results
Visual: Speaker smiles, looks relieved.
Audio: "Fully booked weekends. Waiting lists. And zero time spent worrying about 'what to post today.'"
Text Overlay: Fully booked. Zero stress.

(25–30 sec) CTA
Visual: Speaker taps wrist where a watch would be.
Audio: "Spots are filling fast. Book a consultation call right now—link is in the bio."
Text Overlay: Limited Slots ⏰ Book Now
