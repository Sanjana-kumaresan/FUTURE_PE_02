AI Content Marketing using UGC Ads – Task 2

📌 Project Overview

This project builds a structured prompt engineering system to generate high-converting UGC-style advertisements for Social Media Management services. The goal is to create platform-native, conversion-focused content tailored for small business owners in India who struggle to generate leads and engagement through Instagram.

📌 Business / Service Chosen

Service: Social Media Management (Instagram growth, content strategy, engagement optimization)

Target Audience: Small business owners in India

Goal: Generate consultation bookings and qualified enquiries

📌 Prompt Engineering Strategy

This project applies structured prompt design techniques including:

Role-based prompting (Acting as a performance marketer and UGC creator)

Structured content framework (Hook → Problem → Solution → Benefits → CTA)

Platform adaptation prompting for Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube Shorts

Conversion-focused messaging design targeting real business pain points

📌 Outputs Generated

15 scroll-stopping hooks

3–5 Instagram UGC ad scripts

Facebook Feed adaptations

YouTube Shorts adaptations

All outputs are documented inside the outputs/ folder.

📌 Key Learnings

Crafting scroll-stopping hooks using pain-point psychology

Structuring AI prompts for marketing-focused outputs

Adapting tone and CTA based on platform behavior

Understanding engagement vs. conversion strategy

Designing AI workflows for real-world digital marketing use
